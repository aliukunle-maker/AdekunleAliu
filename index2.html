Professional Summary

About Me

I am a Civil Engineer and researcher with a PhD in Civil Engineering from the University of KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa, specialising in sustainable concrete technology, water treatment, alternative cementitious materials, and the application of artificial intelligence in the construction industry.

With over 18 years of teaching experience in higher education, I have supervised 15+ student research projects and authored more than 20 peer-reviewed publications amassing 81 citations (h-index: 5). My expertise spans structural engineering, innovative construction materials, and the utilisation of agricultural waste as sustainable binder replacements in concrete production.

I am particularly passionate about bridging machine learning with concrete science — developing GUI-based models for concrete strength prediction and advancing green building materials for environmental sustainability in Africa and beyond.
